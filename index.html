Program Overview

IT ABCDE Lab in Secondary Schools (ABC5) cultivates interest for secondary students in ABCDE: AI & Machine Learning, Blockchain & Cryptography, Cloud Computing, Data Science, and Engineering and Applied Sciences.

Complementary

Fundamental modules are complementary to the existing mathematics and science curriculum for secondary schools from the Education Bureau

Comprehensive

Topics are consistent with university-level and continuous professional development programs but tailored for consumption by secondary-level learners

Complete

All programs teach practical skills with project components for practicing new skills so students are ready to apply for top schools and jobs with recognised certifications

AI & Data Science

A and D stands for AI and data science. In this program, students will expand on the practicality of their mathematics knowledge for the data science applications, especially machine learning (ML).

Learn more

Blockchain & Cloud Computing

B and C stands for blockchain and cryptography and cloud computing. In this program, students will learn about the fundamental building blocks of blockchain technology and practical blockchain applications.

Program Philosophy

IT ABCDE Lab in Secondary Schools (ABC5) is inspired by programs from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, University of Waterloo, and Yale University

Train

From fundamentals to practical levels, mentored from academic and industry experts

Test

Evaluate via practical exams and whitepapers, earn participation and certification certificates

Try

Apply knowledge to a practical capstone project, combining personal interest to build a prototype

Trial

Expand horizons and networks through participating in relevant local and global competitions

The Actual IT ABCDE Lab

Working with our partners, we provide a secure and scalable sandbox for our learners to practice their skills and even create something useful for their alma mater.

Hardware Agnostic

With the latest virtual machine technology, this lab is self-contained and able to run on any networked computer and any computer network, including your school's system.

Practical Exercises

Unlike other so-called "labs" that only install software, our Lab includes templates for students to work off of, which may even be useful in the real world.

Production Environment

Unlike other so-called "labs" that only install software, our Lab includes everything needed for production-ready blockchain and data science applications.
