Our Competition

Hosted at City University of Hong Kong since 2018 and sponsored by HSBC since 2020,
the International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL) is a world-renowned and -recognized competition.

International Exposure

IBCOL attracts contestants from the top schools from dozens of countries with support of local governments and guilds, such as ministry-level and legislative representatives from information & communication technology, the privacy commission, and academic "celebrities" of their respective fields of computer science, engineering, etc.

International Collaboration

IBCOL contestants build a life-long friendship and start working relationships through initiatives and working groups, such as Immunodex for healthcare and CredProof for education.

International Acknowledgement

IBCOL participants regardless of performance at the competition are sought-after by banks, schools, and tech companies for internships and offers around the world.

Our Courses

Our Lab

Working with our partners, we provide a secure and scalable sandbox for our learners to practice their skills and even create something useful for their alma mater.

Hardware Agnostic

It is self-contained and is able to run on any networked computer and any computer network.

Ready-to-code Exercises

Unlike other so-called "labs" that only install software, our Lab includes templates for students to work off of, which may even be useful in the real world.

Production Environment

Unlike other so-called "labs" that only install software, our Lab includes the cryptographic and governance infrastructure for production-ready blockchain applications.
