OGCIO to speak about IT Innovation Lab at ABC5-Lab Seminar

26 Nov 2020

Brian Sun from OGCIO is invited to HKBCS to speak about the IT Innovation Lab funding on Dec 4. The OGCIO implements the “IT Innovation Lab in Secondary Schools” (IT Innovation Lab) initiative under the existing “Enriched IT Programme in Secondary Schools” (EITP) to provide funding support of up to $1 million in the three school years from 2020/21 to 2022/23 for each publicly funded secondary school to upgrade their IT equipment and facilities and organise IT-related activities. The recorded webinar will be uploaded on Dec 5.

Hong Kong Blockchain Olympiad Officially Begins 2021 Season

8 Oct 2020

Yesterday, HSBC kicked off the 2021 Hong Kong Blockchain Olympiad at CityU and online yesterday. The competition, in its 4th year, is estimated to receive more than 60 student proposals, with a selected team to be mentored by expert blockchain advisors and academics throughout the competition.

The winning teams will be invited to join the International Blockchain Olympiad 2020, competing with students from global chapters. This is the first year to have a secondary school division for students in Hong Kong secondary students of all ages. The 2021 Hong Kong Blockchain Olympiad is funded by HSBC for the second time in a row and supported by the College of Business at the City University of Hong Kong (CityU), Hong Kong Blockchain Society (HKBCS), and Office of the Government Chief Information Officer of the Hong Kong SAR Government (OGCIO).

The Five levels of ABCD (ABC5-Lab)

17 July 2020

We are happy to announce that we presented our IT-programme, The Five Levels of ABCD (ABC5-Lab), on The Future Tech Talent Theme Day on 16 July. ABC5-Lab is a mixed-mode and pandemic-proof education programme from the Hong Kong Blockchain Society that prepares local secondary school students for enriched IT learning experience and competitions in AI, blockchain, and computing — supported by the OGCIO. ABC5-Lab is fully-subsidized by the IT Innovation Lab programme.

The Future Tech Talent Theme Day

28 Nov 2019

15 Nov - 2019 - HKBCS is pleased to announce that HKTDC and the OGCIO will co-organise a Future Tech Talent Theme Day (tentative name) on 2 January 2020, which will be the launch day of the HKTDC 30th Education and Careers Expo.

OGCIO aims to enhance secondary students’ interest in IT learning and IT careers and raise public’s awareness of digital transformation taking place in all walks of life.

The Expo is one of the largest education and career fairs in Hong Kong for students to obtain the latest information about academic courses and the working market. It was attended by more than 100 000 visitors, including around 100 group visits from schools in 2019. The target audience is youth, post-graduates, graduates, associate degree graduates, secondary students, school representatives and the general public. It will be the 30th Edition of the Expo in 2020 and aims to provide a wealth of information on education and job opportunities for students and visitors at different stages of life and career paths to better equip themselves.
