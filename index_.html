1. Our Competition

Hosted at City University of Hong Kong since 2018 and sponsored by HSBC since 2020, the International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL) is a world-renowned and -recognized competition.

  • International exposure: IBCOL attracts contestants from the top schools from dozens of countries with support of local governments and guilds, such as ministry-level and legislative representatives from information & communication technology, the privacy commission, and academic "celebrities" of their respective fields of computer science, engineering, etc.
  • International collaboration: IBCOL contestants build a life-long friendship and start working relationships through initiatives and working groups, such as Immunodex for healthcare and CredProof for education.
  • International acknowledgement: IBCOL participants regardless of performance at the competition are sought-after by banks, schools, and tech companies for internships and offers around the world.
