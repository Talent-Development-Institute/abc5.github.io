Workshops

The Capstone Project Ideation Workshops encourages students to think about Blockchain and AI in fields of personal interest and real world applications. These ideation workshops are held in an open floor style with industry and academic experts so that ideas can be discussed and openly evaluated. Teachers are welcome to observe.

To join our zoom workshops, please register to get the passcode.

If you have further inquiries, please contact us via WhatsApp or email (hello@abc5.org)
